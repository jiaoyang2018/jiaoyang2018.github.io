This course covers advanced econometrics and R programming for Master's students, with a focus on causal inference methods widely used in empirical research. As a Teaching Assistant, I led lab sessions teaching students how to implement Difference-in-Differences (DID), Instrumental Variables (IV), Regression Discontinuity (RD), and matching estimators in R, helping them build both theoretical understanding and practical skills for rigorous causal analysis.
